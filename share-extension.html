What is Share Extension?

Share Extension allows for text (max 200 characters) or URLs be shared over to Sorted.
To access this click on the share icon and select Sorted.


Click on the Share Icon and Select Sorted



The Sorted share menu will be shown. The content can be shared to a specific list or a reminder time can be applied to the content and it will be shown on the schedule.
