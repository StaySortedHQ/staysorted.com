Schedule tasks, faster.

Having a plan is great. Setting "one reminder" at a time is not. Studies show that scheduled tasks are more likely to get done. So setup a plan in seconds with OneReminder's direct, fun and patented gestures.

Adapt to change, faster.

Your day changes. Your schedule should too. Don't get stuck with a stale schedule or be forced to update "one reminder" at a time.

With OneReminder, sweep to multi-select in a single stroke. Scroll the Time Ruler to offset your reminders. Press and hold to reorder. Tap the calendar to reschedule. Update your schedule on-the-fly and stay on top of your day.

Organise lists, faster.

Organise your ideas and tasks in Smart Lists. Schedule them with a tap. Use sophisticated gestures and advanced functionality to plan your day. Productivity at the speed of swipes.

Sync with iCloud.

Sync your reminders with iCloud and have them with you on all your devices*. Convenient. Secure. Ready for you anywhere and anytime.

* OneReminder for Mac is in the works.
